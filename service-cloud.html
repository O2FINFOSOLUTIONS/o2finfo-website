SaaS Salesforce

Salesforce implementation can massively improve workforce productivity and customer satisfaction. However, it’s a complex and time-consuming process, and quite expensive if you’re doing it without prior experience. We bring you our years of experience to deliver quality Salesforce Consulting services quickly and cost-effectively, at minimal risk.


Our Salesforce Implementation services include

  • In-depth Strategy: We will work with you to evaluate your existing practices and systems, recommend courses of action, develop practices, and create a Salesforce Consulting strategy.
  • Cloud Setup for Sales and Service: We will take up full responsibility for the setup, configuration and customization of Salesforce to support your business needs. We will incorporate workflows, email templates, and approval processes into your business process automation. We will work with you to create highly effective custom reports and dashboards to afford you greater insight into your business.
  • Custom Development: We bring you years of experience in developing custom features for standard Salesforce applications. We use an extremely powerful, scalable and secure cloud computing platform which enables us to develop products at much lower costs and greater speeds. We can help you accelerate product development while saving you costs, which should be a welcome solution. Our Salesforce platform services include Discovery, Prototype, Development and Launch.

Why Choose O2F as Your Salesforce Partner

  • Reliability: We have earned a satisfactory customer satisfaction score on Salesforce.com and reliable reviews for our service on AppExchange.
  • Experience: We have delivered several successful salesforce.com projects, backed by strong customer references.
  • Affordability: We offer you all the attention and thoroughness you require, without the overheads and additional costs of larger consulting companies. We offer a winning combination of onshore-offshore delivery to cut costs without compromising on the quality.

For example, if you have existing such ERP applications, marketing automation software, financial software and call center software and more, we’re here to make sure they’re all integrated within Salesforce.
